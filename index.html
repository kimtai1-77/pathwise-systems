Case Study 1:
Covering Revenue Loopholes in a Bike E-Commerce Store

4 Areas of fixing revenue loss

1. Adding an action to - 'out of stock'

User State
"I want this, but I can't do anything because it's out of stock"
The Risk
When users can't take action — those sales are lost immediately
The Fix
Create an action (notify me) that captures demand that would have otherwise been lost
Business Value
Captures demand that would otherwise be lost.

2. Prompting users to make decisions quickly instead of delaying

User State
"Do I buy now or come back later"
Business Risk
No urgency means buyers postpone — and postponed decisions rarely convert
The Fix
Move buyers to act now by creating urgency (limited stock, sale ending soon, etc)
Business Value
Moves buyers to act immediately instead of delaying

Removing guesswork on which 'uncertain' items to stock

User State
"Since I'm not in a hurry I might as well preorder and get a discount"
Business Risk
Locking capital by making stock decisions without clear demand
The Fix
Make informed stocking decisions on uncertain products
Business Value
Captures demand early and informs what to stock
